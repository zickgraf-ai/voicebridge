What TapToSpeak Is

TapToSpeak is a text-to-speech communication tool designed to help patients who are temporarily unable to speak. It converts typed or selected text into spoken audio using premium AI voices (via OpenAI) or your device's built-in speech engine (Web Speech API).

What TapToSpeak Is Not

TapToSpeak is not a medical device. It is not a substitute for professional medical care, clinical communication systems, or emergency services. TapToSpeak does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, contact emergency services immediately.

The pre-loaded phrases in TapToSpeak are provided for convenience only and should not be relied upon as the sole means of communicating critical medical information to healthcare providers.

Third-Party Services

TapToSpeak uses third-party AI services to provide premium features:

  • OpenAI — for premium text-to-speech voice generation. The text you speak is sent to OpenAI's API when using premium voices.
  • Anthropic — for smart phrase suggestions. Contextual information (time of day, location label, recent phrases, medical condition, medications, family member names) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API.

Your use of these features is also subject to the terms and privacy policies of these third-party providers.
