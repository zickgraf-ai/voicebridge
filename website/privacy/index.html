Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

The short version: TapToSpeak does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What data does TapToSpeak collect?

TapToSpeak does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, login, email address, or any form of registration. We do not collect names, health information, usage analytics, device identifiers, location data, or any other personal data.

What happens to the text I type?

The text you type into TapToSpeak is processed entirely on your device using Apple's built-in text-to-speech engine (AVSpeechSynthesizer). Your text is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. When you close the app or clear a message, the text exists only in your device's local memory and is not persisted.

Does TapToSpeak use any third-party services?

No. TapToSpeak does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. The app contains no third-party SDKs that collect data.

Does TapToSpeak access the internet?

No. TapToSpeak works entirely offline. The app does not make any network requests. No data is sent to or received from any server.

Does TapToSpeak access the microphone, camera, or other device features?

TapToSpeak only uses your device's speaker (audio output) to speak text aloud. It does not access your microphone, camera, contacts, photos, location, or any other device sensors or data.

Children's Privacy

TapToSpeak does not collect any data from any user, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Retention

There is no data to retain. TapToSpeak stores no information on any server. Any text you type exists only temporarily in the app's local memory on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL (taptospeak.app/privacy) with an updated “Last updated” date. Since we collect no data and have no user accounts, we have no way to notify users directly — please check this page periodically.
