Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use TapToSpeak?

Type your message in the text field and tap the speak button. The app will read your message aloud through your device's speaker. You can also tap any of the pre-loaded phrase buttons to speak common messages instantly.

Does TapToSpeak work without internet?

Yes. TapToSpeak works entirely offline. No internet connection is required.

Can I change the voice or speaking speed?

TapToSpeak uses your device's system voice settings. To change the voice or speed, go to your device's Settings > Accessibility > Spoken Content (or Settings > Accessibility > VoiceOver > Speech, depending on your device). Changes to your system voice settings will be reflected in TapToSpeak.

Does TapToSpeak collect my data?

No. TapToSpeak does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device. See our full Privacy Policy.

Is TapToSpeak free?

Yes. TapToSpeak is free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscription.

What devices does TapToSpeak support?

TapToSpeak is available for iPhone and iPad. It requires iOS 16.0 or later.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

We'd love to hear from you. Email support@taptospeak.app with a description of the issue or your suggestion.

Can hospitals or clinics use TapToSpeak with patients?

Yes. TapToSpeak is designed for use by patients and caregivers in clinical and home settings. If you're a healthcare provider interested in deploying TapToSpeak across your organization, contact jeff@taptospeak.app.
